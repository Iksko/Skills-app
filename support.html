Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

What is the purpose of the skills recording app?

arrow_forward_ios

The skills recording app is designed to help users track and manage their acquired skills and competencies over time.

How can I add a new skill to my profile?

arrow_forward_ios

To add a new skill, navigate to your profile settings and find the option to add a skill. Provide the skill name, proficiency level, and any additional details you'd like to include.

Can I edit or update the proficiency level of a skill?

arrow_forward_ios

Yes, you can easily edit or update the proficiency level of a skill in your profile settings. Locate the skill you want to modify and adjust the proficiency level accordingly.

Is it possible to set privacy settings for my recorded skills?

arrow_forward_ios

Absolutely. Our app offers privacy settings that allow you to choose who can view your recorded skills. You can keep them private, share them with connections, or make them public.

How do I remove a skill from my profile?

arrow_forward_ios

To remove a skill from your profile, go to your profile settings, find the skill you want to delete, and use the delete option provided.

Are there any tools for visualizing my skill development?

arrow_forward_ios

Absolutely. Our app offers visualizations and graphs that display your skill development over time, helping you visualize your progress.

Is there a limit to the number of skills I can record?

arrow_forward_ios

There is no strict limit to the number of skills you can record. Feel free to add as many skills as you'd like to track your diverse competencies.
